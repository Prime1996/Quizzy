BCB was found in 1972.
It stands for Bangladesh Cricket Board.
It was known as Bangladesh Cricket Control Board at the time of establishment.
It became an associate member of ICC in 1977.
On 26 June 2000, it became a full member.
Professor Mohammad Yousuf Ali was the first president of BCB.
He was the president form 1972 to 1976.
Nazmul Hassan Papon is the president of the organization.
Its operating income is US$ 29 million.
Mashrafi Mortaza was the most successful captain of bangladesh cricket by far.
