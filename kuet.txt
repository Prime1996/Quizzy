Khulna Engineering College was established in 1967.
Later it became BIT Khulna.
In 2003 it was recognized as a university.
The university is situated at Fulbarigate, Khulna.
Currently there are 3 faculties.
There are 16 departments under these faculties offering BSc in Engineering/BURP degrees.
The current intake of the organization is 1065 students for Bachelors degree.
Professor Dr. Quazi Sazzad Hossain is the current VC.
Currently there are 5240 students studying here.
The Student Welfare Department is responsible for the wellbeing of the students.
